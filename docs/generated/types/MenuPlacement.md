# MenuPlacement

Placement of the `HxMenu` relative to its toggle. Physical placements (`Top`, `Bottom`, `Left`, `Right`) are fixed directions regardless of text direction. Logical placements (`Start`, `End`) automatically flip based on RTL. All placements support `-Start` and `-End` alignment modifiers. .
